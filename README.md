# MonieFlow

**Zero Broke Days. Built for us.**

I'm a university student in Nigeria. I know what it feels like to have skills, have time, and still have nothing to show for it at the end of the month. The gig platforms that exist were built for people in San Francisco, not for a 200-level student in Lagos trying to earn honest money between lectures.

MonieFlow is my answer to that.

It's an AI-powered gig and micro-job platform built specifically for young Nigerians — connecting real skills to real opportunities, with fair pay and instant payment. No middlemen taking cuts. No waiting till month-end. No guessing if the client is going to ghost you.

## What It Does

**AI FairMatch™ Engine**
The AI doesn't just throw random gigs at you. It learns your skills, your location, your availability — and matches you to opportunities that actually fit. No more applying to 40 things and hearing nothing back.

**Instant Payments**
Finish the job, get paid. Straight to your MonieFlow wallet. That's it.

**Micro-Upskilling**
2-minute AI-powered lessons that actually move the needle. Learn something new, unlock better-paying gigs, level up over time.

**Trust & Safety**
Client funds are held in escrow before work begins. You work knowing the money is already there. Zero risk.

## Why I Built It

Because I needed it. Because my friends needed it. Because there are thousands of young Nigerians with real, marketable skills who are still broke because the system wasn't built for them.

MonieFlow is for us.

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js
- **Backend:** Firebase
- **AI:** Google Gemini API
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui
- **Hosting:** Vercel
